World Class Pollachi Coconuts

Located in the heart of Zamin Uthukuli, Pollachi, we are a leading supplier and exporter of high-grade coconuts. Pollachi is globally renowned for its ideal climate and red soil, which produces coconuts that are superior in taste, size, and shelf life.

Much like the renowned Amudhasurabi standards, we ensure that every coconut we supply is handpicked from the best farms. Our coconuts are famous for their high water content, thick and sweet kernel (meat), and high oil percentage. We are committed to delivering freshness and quality to our customers across India and overseas.

  • High oil content and thick, sweet meat
  • Direct procurement from Zamin Uthukuli farmers
  • Strict quality grading for size and weight

Organic Farming

Selective Harvesting
