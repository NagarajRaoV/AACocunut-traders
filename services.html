PROCESSING PROCEDURE

Ensuring Excellence in Every Coconut

Our Process

At AA Coconut Enterprises, we maintain the highest standards of quality. Our processing involves:

Sourcing: Direct from Zamin Uthukuli farms.
De-Husking: Careful manual husking to protect the shell.
Grading: Sorting by size (12-13 inches) and weight.
Packing: Using breathable PP/Gunny bags for export.

Quality Control

We inspect every single coconut for cracks, water sound, and freshness before it leaves our facility. This ensures that our customers receive only A-Grade Pollachi coconuts with long shelf life and high oil content.

Packaging Standards

To prevent damage during transit, we use high-strength packaging materials. We offer customized bag sizes (25 nuts or 50 nuts per bag) to suit the needs of wholesalers and exporters alike.
